CLOCKOUT COMEDY

What we are

Clock Out Comedy is a comedy show that boosts the 9 to 5. In today's professional world, many companies are looking to improve company morale and innovative ways to bring their team closer together.

At Clock Out Comedy, we see the importance of a healthy work environment and want to help your company achieve that goal by providing your employees with an hour long comedic variety show packed with Chicago's top comedians.

Our Founder

Amber Autry is a Standup Comedian, Producer, Writer and Actor in Chicago. She produces several shows in the city along with Clock Out Comedy. Amber started Clock Out Comedy because she saw how the 9 to 5 corporate lifestyle can be draining, leaving little energy for employees to enjoy themselves after work. Amber has always had a passion for laughing with people and spreading joy and is thrilled to do it for a living.
